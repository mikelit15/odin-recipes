Stromboli

Home

Description

Ingredients

  • Pizza Crust
  • Lean ground beef
  • Jar pizza sauce
  • Cooked ham
  • Green bell pepper
  • Red onion
  • Can mushroom
  • Packaged sliced pepperoni
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Cook and stir ground beef in a skillet
  3. Roll dough on a lightly floured surface
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown
